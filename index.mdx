Proprietas is the modern workspace for UK estates teams. It replaces legacy CAFM/IWMS tools (Planon, MRI Evolution, Concerto, QFM) with one place for **statutory compliance**, **lease intelligence** and **day-to-day facilities**.

Built for multi-academy trusts, care and hotel groups, managing agents, and NHS, university and large estates teams — anyone carrying personal liability for a portfolio's statutory compliance.

## Why it works differently

Legacy CAFM was built for procurement, not for the person who carries the liability. Long implementations, training courses, per-seat fees for every contractor, and interfaces from another decade.

Proprietas inverts that:

- **Ten-minute onboarding**, not a six-month project
- **AI reads your documents** — drop a certificate or a lease and it files itself against the right property and regime
- **Unlimited contractor and tenant access**, free at every tier
- **Published pricing** — every number on one page, no "contact sales"
- **Mobile-first** — contractors live on their phones, so public flows are built for one
