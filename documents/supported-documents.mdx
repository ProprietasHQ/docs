Proprietas recognises and extracts structured data from the documents an estates team actually handles. Drop any of these and the right fields are pulled, page-cited, and filed.

## Compliance Certificates

All certificates share a common shape — issue date, expiry date, certificate number, inspector and inspecting body, outcome, and any findings.

| Document | Pulls |
| --- | --- |
| Fire Risk Assessment (FRA) | Dates, risk level, findings with priority & deadline |
| EICR (electrical) | Dates, inspector, C1/C2/C3 observations |
| Gas Safety (CP12) | Dates, Gas Safe engineer & number |
| L8 / Legionella risk assessment | Dates, risk level, monitoring actions |
| Asbestos survey | Dates, register, re-inspection schedule |
| PAT | Dates, appliance results |
| LOLER report | Dates, examiner, defects |
| EPC | Rating, dates |
| F-Gas | Dates, refrigerant check |
| TM44 air-conditioning | Dates, inspector |
| Fire alarm certificate | Dates, system, defects |
| Emergency lighting certificate | Dates, test result |
| Fire extinguisher, sprinkler, dry riser | Dates, service result |
| PSSR (pressure systems) | Dates, examiner |
| DSEAR assessment | Dates, risk findings |
| HMO licence | Issue & expiry dates, licence number, issuing council, permitted occupancy |
| Insurance policy | Dates, cover type, amount |

## Leases

A full commercial lease yields the fields that matter for portfolio management, each page-cited:

- **Term** — start, end, rent-commencement date (distinct, so rent-free periods are handled)
- **Parties** — landlord, tenant, counterparty, other named parties
- **Rent** — annual rent, currency, premium, VAT applicability
- **Break clauses** — date, notice period, who may exercise
- **Rent reviews** — date and basis (open-market, RPI, CPI, fixed)
- **Service charge** — cap percentage and basis
- **Repairing obligation** — FRI / IRI
- **Alienation regime** — assignment / underletting rules
- **LTA 1954 status** — security of tenure excluded vs retained
- **Permitted use** — planning use class and reference
- **Deposit** — amount and months covered
- **Area** — square feet / metres
- **Title number** — HM Land Registry

See [Lease intelligence](/leases/lease-intelligence) for how this surfaces in the app.

## Deeds & Transfers

Lease-adjacent documents are read for their parties, effective date, premises and a plain-English summary of what they do:

- Licence to alter
- Deed of variation
- Rent review memorandum
- Assignment
- Surrender
- Wayleave
- **TR1** (HM Land Registry transfer of whole) — with title number and consideration

See [Deeds & transfers](/leases/deeds-and-transfers).

## Other Documents

Insurance policies, contractor registrations, meeting minutes, invoices, work-order reports and photo reports are recognised and filed; anything unrecognised is kept as a general document so nothing is lost.
