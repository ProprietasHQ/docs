Contractors are the people who do the work. Proprietas keeps a directory of them, tracks their competence, and controls where they're visible.

## The Directory

Each contractor record holds the company details, trades (gas, electrical, fire safety, plumbing, general), a primary contact, insurance details and status (active / inactive / suspended). Sensitive personal details are encrypted at rest.

## Competence Certificates

A contractor's **competence certificates** — Gas Safe, NICEIC, SafeContractor, CHAS, ECA — are tracked with their numbers and expiry dates. This is what powers competence gating on [work orders](/facilities/work-orders#competence-gating): an expired ticket can block the contractor from being assigned new work until it's renewed, so you don't accidentally put an out-of-ticket trade on a regulated job.

## Org-Wide vs Site-Scoped

A contractor is either:

- **Org-wide** — visible and assignable at every site, or
- **Site-scoped** — visible only at the specific sites you link them to.

Site-scoping keeps a managing agent's per-client contractor lists clean, and stops a site manager seeing trades they'd never use.

## Contacts and Self-Enrolment

A contractor can have several **contacts** (their employees). A contact can link to a login and sign in to the [portal](/facilities/contractor-portal) to see every job their company is engaged on — but they only ever see their own work, enforced at the server, never just hidden in the UI.
