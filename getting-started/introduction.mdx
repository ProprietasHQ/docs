Proprietas is an AI-first property management platform for UK estates teams. It replaces legacy CAFM/IWMS systems (Planon, MRI Evolution, Concerto, QFM) with a modern workspace built around three pillars: **statutory compliance**, **lease intelligence** and **facilities**.

For the full product story start at [Welcome](/). For setup, go to [Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart). For the product stance versus legacy CAFM, see [Philosophy](/overview/philosophy).

Compliance covers FRA, legionella/L8, EICR, gas, LOLER, asbestos, PAT, EPC, F-Gas, TM44, fire alarm, emergency lighting and more. Lease intelligence pulls term dates, break clauses, rent reviews, service-charge caps and the counterparty — each figure linked to the page it came from. Facilities covers issues, planned work and the contractor portal.

## Who it's for

Multi-academy trusts, care-home and hotel groups, managing agents, and NHS, university and large estates departments — anyone carrying personal liability for a portfolio's statutory compliance.

## How access works

Proprietas has three kinds of user:

- **Organisation admins** — billing, members, SSO and settings
- **Editors** — day-to-day estate work (billable seats)
- **Light users** — contractors and tenants (unlimited and free)

See [Roles & invites](/members/roles-and-invites) for the full matrix.
