Proprietas is an AI-first property management platform for UK estates teams. It replaces legacy CAFM/IWMS systems (Planon, MRI Evolution, Concerto, QFM, Asckey) with a modern, mobile-first workspace built around three pillars.

## The Three Pillars

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Statutory compliance" icon="shield-check">
    Every UK regime tracked, with reminders and an audit trail.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Lease intelligence" icon="file-text">
    Lease dates and clauses, read in seconds and page-cited.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Facilities" icon="wrench">
    Issues, work orders and a no-account contractor portal.
  </Card>
</Columns>

Compliance covers FRA, legionella/L8, EICR, gas, LOLER, asbestos, PAT, EPC, F-Gas, TM44, fire alarm, emergency lighting and more. Lease intelligence pulls term dates, break clauses, rent reviews, service-charge caps and the counterparty — each figure linked to the page it came from. Facilities adds issue reporting, planned maintenance and the day-to-day, without the spreadsheet.

## Who It's For

Multi-Academy Trusts, care-home and hotel groups, managing agents, and NHS, university and large estates departments — anyone carrying personal liability for a portfolio's statutory compliance.

## How Access Works

Proprietas has three kinds of user:

| Role | Who | Cost |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Org Admin** | Configures the workspace, manages members and billing | Counts against your seat cap |
| **Editor** | Estates managers, compliance officers, FM admins | Counts against your seat cap |
| **Light** | Contractors, tenants, view-only users | **Free and unlimited at every tier** |

<Note>
  Contractor and tenant access is always free — you're never charged per contractor or per tenant.
</Note>
