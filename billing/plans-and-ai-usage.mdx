Proprietas is published pricing — every tier is on one page, with a single annual/monthly toggle. Manage your subscription under **Settings → Billing**.

## Tiers

| | Compliance | Professional | Enterprise |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Price** | £199/mo annual | £699/mo annual | from £2,499/mo annual |
| **Sites** | 10 | 50 | Unlimited |
| **Editor seats** | 5 | 25 | Unlimited |
| **AI usage** | Fair-use | Higher fair-use | Unlimited |
| **Light users** | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| **Single sign-on** | — | OIDC | OIDC + SAML + SCIM |

<Note>
  Light users — contractors, tenants, viewers — are free and unlimited on every tier.
</Note>

## Switching Tiers

Change tier any time from **Settings → Billing**:

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately, with a prorated charge for the rest of the period.
- **Downgrades** take effect at your next renewal, so you keep what you've paid for.

## AI Usage and Credit Top-Ups

Each lease, certificate or document the AI reads is one **extraction**. Identical files aren't charged twice — a re-read of the same document and schema is served from cache, so you never pay for the same PDF twice.

Every plan includes a generous AI **fair-use** allowance — effectively unlimited for normal use. You're never billed per document and there's no rate card to track:

- See exactly where you are with the **fair-use bars** under Billing — daily and weekly, with a live reset countdown.
- Go beyond the allowance in a busy month and you can **top up AI credit** in one click. Additional usage is then drawn from that credit **at cost** — you pay the underlying AI provider's price for the extra calls, with no markup games.

## Trial and Lapses

New workspaces get a **14-day trial** with no card. If a trial lapses without a subscription, the editor app is paused at a paywall until you subscribe — your data is untouched, and light users keep reporting through the portal.

## Your Data

Files live in UK/EU storage and are served only through short-lived private links. Sensitive personal data is encrypted at the column level. See the [security posture](https://proprietas.app/security) for the full picture.
