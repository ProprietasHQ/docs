## The Trial

New workspaces get a **14-day trial** with **no card required**. You get the full editor experience — drop documents, seed compliance, parse leases — so you can feel the product against your own estate before paying.

A banner on the dashboard shows the days remaining, and you'll get reminders as the trial nears its end (at 7, 3 and 1 days out).

## If a Trial Lapses

If a trial ends without a subscription, the **editor app is paused at a paywall** until you subscribe. Two things stay true:

- **Your data is untouched** — nothing is deleted; subscribing restores full access immediately.
- **Light users keep working** — contractors and tenants can still report issues and use the portal through the lapse.

You'll get an "ended" note shortly after, and a win-back reminder a little later — both easy to act on with one click.

## Subscribing and Switching

Subscribe or change tier any time under **Settings → Billing**:

- **Upgrades** take effect immediately, prorated for the rest of the period.
- **Downgrades** take effect at your next renewal, so you keep what you've paid for.
- Switch between **annual and monthly** whenever you like — annual is roughly 17% cheaper — with the change applying from the next renewal.
