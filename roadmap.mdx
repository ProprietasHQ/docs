An honest picture of where Proprietas is. Everything under **What's live now** works today; everything under **Coming up** is committed but not shipped.

## What's Live Now

### Compliance
- Every UK statutory regime tracked — FRA, EICR, L8/legionella, gas, asbestos, PAT, EPC, F-Gas, TM44, fire alarm, emergency lighting, insurance
- Per-site and per-floor status at a glance — overdue, due soon, valid
- Pre-expiry reminders at 90/60/30/7 days, routed to a named Responsible Person and an escalation contact
- Automatic work orders raised at the 7-day threshold and routed to the right contractor
- One-click audit pack PDF for inspectors (Ofsted, CQC, DSPT, insurers)
- Compliance deadlines you can subscribe to in Google, Outlook or Apple calendar

### Lease Intelligence
- Drop a lease PDF — term dates, break clauses, rent reviews, service-charge caps, alienation, repairing obligation, LTA 1954 status and permitted use, in under a minute
- Every figure page-cited back to the source wording
- Inline editing of any field, with partial saves
- Reads land-registry transfers (TR1) and reversionary documents too

### Facilities
- Issue reporting from a public link — no account needed
- Work orders with a no-account contractor portal
- Contractor competence gating (Gas Safe, NICEIC, SafeContractor, CHAS)
- Mobile-first across every contractor and tenant flow

### AI Assistant & Intake
- Ask your portfolio anything from the topbar or the search bar
- Universal document intake — drop any PDF and it files itself
- Free compliance scanner at [proprietas.app/try](https://proprietas.app/try) — no signup
- Low-confidence statutory dates held for human review, never auto-filed wrong

### Members & Security
- Editor / Light / Admin roles, with light (contractor & tenant) access free and unlimited
- Email invitations and an audit trail on every change
- **Single sign-on (OIDC)** for Google, Microsoft Entra and Okta, with domain verification
- **Domain join-requests** — people on your verified domain request access and an admin approves
- Multi-factor authentication, column-level encryption of sensitive data, signed DPA

### Billing
- Self-serve subscriptions on three published tiers with an annual/monthly toggle
- Switch tiers any time — immediate prorated upgrades, downgrades at renewal
- Transparent AI fair-use bars and one-click credit top-ups, with extra usage drawn at cost

## Coming Up

<Note>These are committed and in progress — not yet live.</Note>

### Production-Grade (Next)
- Enforced MFA for admins with step-up on sensitive actions
- Published 99% accuracy on UK commercial leases, measured every quarter
- Regulator-shaped report bundles — Ofsted, CQC, DSPT, ISO 27001, insurance renewals — one click each
- Native offline contractor mobile app
- REST API and webhooks for everything
- Out-of-the-box integrations — Xero, Sage, IRIS, ShiftPlanning, Planday
- Enterprise SSO (SAML) and automated provisioning (SCIM), with IP allow-listing
- One-afternoon migration from Planon, Concerto and MRI

### International (Later)
- Ireland first, then Germany, France and the Netherlands — local lease formats out of the box
- EU compliance regimes per market
- Per-country language packs and multi-currency rent handling
- Cross-border group-management dashboards
