## What's in a Pack

An audit pack assembles, for a site (or a single obligation):

- Every current certificate with its dates, inspector and outcome
- Outstanding findings and how they were resolved
- The obligation list with computed status
- Recent work orders and contractor competence

Because it's generated from live data, the pack is always current — there's no stale export to regenerate.

## Scope-Aware

Audit packs respect the [active scope](/getting-started/properties-and-scope#the-active-property-scope). At a multi-property site you can pull a pack for the whole building or for one floor — each tenant gets their own evidence bundle.

<Note>
  Audit-pack export is included at **every tier**. It's the product, not a paid add-on.
</Note>

## The Audit Trail Behind It

The pack is trustworthy because every change underneath it is logged. Every compliance, lease, contractor and financial write produces an immutable audit event — actor, before, after, IP and user-agent — in the same transaction as the change. See [Audit trail](/security/audit-trail).
