Notifications are how Proprietas surfaces the things that can't wait for you to go looking — a deadline approaching, a payment overdue, a new issue, someone requesting access.

## The Bell

The notification bell in the top bar shows an unread count and a dot. Open it for your most recent notifications, mark them read, or jump to the full feed. Clicking a notification takes you to the thing it's about — an obligation, a rent payment, a work order — or deep-links straight into the relevant Settings panel (a join request opens Members).

## What You Get Notified About

- **Compliance** — pre-expiry reminders at 90, 60, 30 and 7 days
- **Rent** — due soon and overdue
- **Members** — invitations accepted, new join requests
- **Work orders & issues** — assignments

## Choosing Your Channels

Under **Settings → Notifications**, you control each category independently:

- **In-app** vs **email** per category
- A **weekly digest** option
- **SMS** reserved for the 7-day compliance red line

So a compliance officer can take expiry reminders by email while a manager takes only the weekly digest — without anyone drowning in a shared inbox.

<Tip>
  Reminders route to a regime's **Responsible Person** and an **escalation contact**, not one catch-all address. Set these per site so the right person gets the nudge. See [Reminders & escalation](/compliance/reminders).
</Tip>
