The assistant is a first-class surface in Proprietas, not a chat box stuck in the corner. Ask a question about your estate in plain English and it answers from your live data — then links you to the records behind the answer.

<Frame>

</Frame>

## What You Can Ask

The assistant is grounded in your own portfolio, so the questions that matter are the operational ones:

- *"Which sites have an overdue EICR?"*
- *"When does the lease on the Maple Street site break?"*
- *"Show me every gas safety certificate expiring in the next 60 days."*
- *"What's my total compliance exposure across the trust right now?"*
- *"Draft an audit pack for Oakfield Primary."*

## How It Works

Each answer is built from typed, audit-logged tools — compliance lookups, lease summaries, portfolio queries, audit-pack drafting — not free-form guessing. That means answers link back to the underlying records, and every action the assistant takes on your behalf is recorded in the audit trail like any other change.

<Note>
  Conversations persist. Open the assistant from the sidebar or the topbar, and pick up where you left off — each thread keeps its history.
</Note>

## Where to Find It

The assistant has its own sidebar entry and its own pages, and it's reachable from the topbar on every screen. It respects your active [property scope](/interface/navigating#property-scope) — focus on one site and the assistant answers about that site.
